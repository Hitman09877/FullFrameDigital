# Full Frame Digital — Pricing Reference (Internal)

All rates in AUD and include GST (10%). Tax provisioning at 30% is factored into all pricing.

---

## Rate Calculation Method

| Step | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Start with take-home rate | e.g. $150/hr |
| Add 30% tax provision | ÷ 0.70 = $214.29 |
| Add 10% GST | × 1.10 = $235.71 |
| Half day | Hourly × 4 hours × 0.90 (10% discount) |
| Full day | Hourly × 8 hours × 0.90 (10% discount) |

## Retainer Package Tiers (Real Estate Clients)

### Tier 1 — "Listings" | ~$1,500-2,000/mo
- 2x property photo shoots/mo
- 1x short-form video per listing
- Basic editing, 48hr turnaround
- Digital delivery via shared drive
- Target: individual agents needing consistent property content

### Tier 2 — "Brand Builder" | ~$3,000-4,000/mo (Recommended)
- 4x property shoots/mo
- 2x short-form videos/mo
- 1x agent personal brand content session/mo (BTS, talking head, lifestyle)
- Social media content calendar + caption writing
- Graphic design for social posts (templates + custom)
- Monthly strategy call
- Target: agents/small agencies building a brand

### Tier 3 — "Full Frame" | ~$5,000-7,000/mo
- Unlimited property shoots (reasonable scope)
- Drone/aerial content
- Full social media management (posting, engagement, reporting)
- Quarterly brand strategy review
- Website updates/maintenance
- Priority scheduling + same-day turnaround available
- Monthly performance report
- Target: agencies/top agents wanting market dominance

### Retainer Pricing Notes
- One-off rate card prices sit slightly above equivalent retainer rates to incentivise recurring
- 10-15% discount for 6 or 12-month commitments
- $500-1,000 one-time onboarding/setup fee for new retainer clients
